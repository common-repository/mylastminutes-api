== Description ==

So you want to use our plugin on your Wordpress website? We have you covered! [Mylastminutes](https://www.mylastminutes.nl/api "Mylastminutes API") gives you al the last minutes you want on your website.
Create a free account and start creating your desired top 10 last minute lists with your . Put it with a simple shortcode on your Wordpress pages, widgets or posts.
